Christmas in Romania

Here in Romania, we do things differently – Christmas is a time for warm traditions, family reunions, and festive spirit that fills the air. From the melodic carols echoing through the streets to the vibrant markets adorned with lights, our celebrations are steeped in history and heartwarming customs. Whether it's enjoying a hearty meal with loved ones or participating in lively festivities, Christmas in Romania is an unforgettable experience of joy and togetherness.

Romanians go skiing?

Yes, we do! Skiing in Romania is a beloved tradition, offering stunning locations perfect for winter sports enthusiasts. From the Carpathian Mountains to the stunning hills of Transylvania, our ski resorts cater to all levels of experience. One of the most popular destinations is Poiana Brașov, renowned for its modern ski slopes and breathtaking views. It’s a favorite among both locals and tourists alike, offering everything from beginner runs to more advanced challenges.

About the carolers

In Romania, caroling isn't just about singing – it's also about "rituals". Groups of young people go from house to house, singing festive carols not only for entertainment but also as a blessing for health and prosperity in the coming year. The carolers are often rewarded with sweets, nuts, and money – making the tradition just as much about spreading joy as it is about receiving a little something in return! In some areas, carolers even dress up in colorful costumes and traditional masks, turning the entire event into a fun, festive spectacle!
